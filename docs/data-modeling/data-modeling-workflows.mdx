# Data Modeling Workflows

Learn how to iterate on your data model, add semantic information, and more.

## Iterate on your data model

When your underlying data model changes, you can iterate on your data model to reflect the changes.

[Learn more about iterating on your data model](/data-modeling/iterate.mdx)

## Add semantic information

When you add semantic information to your data model, you can help the PromptQL understand your data and create
appropriate query plans.
